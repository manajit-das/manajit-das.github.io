About

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computational Chemistry at IIT Bombay, where I have the privilege of working in Prof. R. B. Sunoj's lab. My research focuses on exploring the intricate mechanisms of chemical reactions using advanced computational techniques. Beyond my academic endeavors, I am passionate about data analysis, teaching, and engaging in interdisciplinary research collaborations.
